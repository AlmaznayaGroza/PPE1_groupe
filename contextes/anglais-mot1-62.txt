   Collective consciousness, collective conscience, or collective
   conscious (French: conscience collective) is the set of shared beliefs,
   ideas, and moral attitudes which operate as a unifying force within
   society.^[1] In general, it does not refer to the specifically moral
   conscience, but to a shared understanding of social norms.^[2]
